Chocolate Chip Cookie

Serves: 12 cookies

Ingredients

  • 8 tablespoons of salted butter
  • 1/2 cup white sugar or raw cane sugar
  • 1/4 packed light brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 egg
  • 1 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350ËšF. Microwave the butter for about 40 seconds to just barely melt it.
  2. Using a stand mixer or electric beaters, beat the butter with the sugars until creamy.
  3. Add the vanilla and the egg; beat on low speed until just incorporated - 10-15 seconds or so.
  4. Add the flour, baking soda, and salt. Mix until crumbles form.
  1. Use your hands to press the crumbles together into a dough. It should form one large ball that is easy to handle (right at the stage between "wet" dough and "dry" dough).
  2. Add the chocolate chips and incorporate with your hands.
  3. Roll the dough into 12 large balls (or 9 for HUGELY awesome cookies) and place on a cookie sheet.
  4. Bake for 9-11 minutes until the cookies look puffy and dry and just barely golden.
  5. Let them cool on the pan for a good 30 minutes or so.
