Super Moist Pumpkin Bread

Serves: 2 loaves

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 2 cups cane sugar
  • 1 15-ounce can pumpkin
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 3 1/2 all purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup turbinado cane sugar

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350ËšF.
  2. Whisk the eggs, oils, and sugar together until smooth. Add the pumpkin, milk, and water - whisk until smooth.
  3. Stir in the flour, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon.
  4. Transfer batter to two 9x5inch loaf pans lined with parchment paper. Bake for 35 minutes.
  5. Remove from oven, brush with melted butter and sprinkle with turbinado sugar, and return to oven for another 5-10 minutes to set the sugar.
  6. The bread should be golden brown and firm to the touch when done, and a toothpick inserted should come out clean.
  7. Let cool slightly before slicing and serving. Store in the refrigerator.
