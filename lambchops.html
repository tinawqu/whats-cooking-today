Garlic and Anchovy Roasted Lamb Chops & Sage Browned Butter Sauce

Serves: 6

Ingredients

  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 anchovy fillets, minced
  • 1 teaspoon anchovy oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon cracked black pepper
  • 6 American Lamb Chops, room temperature
  • 1/4 cup (1/2stick) unsalted butter
  • 10 castelvetrano olives, pitted and chopped
  • 8 fresh sage leaves
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 ËšF.
  2. Place garlic, anchovies, anchovy oil, and black pepper in a small bowl and whisk together.
  3. Lightly season lamb chops with salt and pepper.
  4. Brush garlic-anchovy mixture over each chop, on both sides.
  1. Pour olive oil into a large saute pan and place over medium-high heat.
  2. Sear each chop in the pan for 2 to 3 minutes, on each side, and transfer to a roasting pan.
  3. Roast lamb chops for about 13 to 15 minutes for medium-rare, and 15 to 18 minutes for medium doneness. Remove from the oven and allow chops to rest for 5 to 7 minutes.
  4. Using the same pan you used to sear the chops, melt the butter over medium-low heat. Allow butter to cook for 6 to 8 minutes or until butter has melted and begins to foam and brown. Add olives and sage leaves and gently fry for 1 to 2 minutes. Remove mixture from the heat. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Transfer chops onto a serving dish/platter and pour butter sauce over chops. Serve immediately.
